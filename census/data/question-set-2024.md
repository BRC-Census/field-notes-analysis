# 2024 Burning Man Field Notes Survey Instrument

## Question Set A - Transformation
1. What is your age?
2. What is your gender?
3. Where do you reside in the default world?
4. How many times have you been to Burning Man (including this time)?
5. Did Burning Man change you? When? How? Please explain.
6. Did you expect to be changed by Burning Man? Why or why not?
7. If Burning Man changed you, did you experience the change as sudden, gradual, or both? Please explain.
8. Did Burning Man change the way you relate to other people? How?
9. If Burning Man changed you, can you describe which factor(s) contributed to the change you experienced?

## Question Set G - Drinking and Smoking
1. What is your age?
2. What is your gender?
3. Where do you reside in the default world?

5. Do you drink and/or smoke at Burning Man? If no, please explain why not, then skip to question 7.
6. If yes, what do you drink/smoke at Burning Man and how often? Is this more, less, or about the same as the amount you drink/smoke in the default world? Please explain why you might behave differently here.

8. If cannabis and other marijuana products were to become completely legal where you reside, please describe what (if anything) would change for you and your friends/family/community?
9. If and when you have used cannabis/marijuana in your lifetime, how does it affect your use of other substances and medicines?

## Question Set M - Sustainability
1. What is your age?
2. What is your gender?
3. Where do you reside in the default world?
4. How many times have you been to Burning Man (including this time)?
5. When you were preparing to attend Burning Man this year, how did concerns about sustainability factor into your choices?
6. Have you seen any sustainable practices at Burning Man that you want to implement for yourself or your camp, such as gray water management, solar power, or non-meat based meals? If so, what and how?
7. Are you interested in making your next burn more sustainable? If so, how? If not, why not?
8. Should Burning Man be a place where people are encouraged to try new sustainable behaviors, even if they require personal sacrifice? Why, or why not?
9. Would a strong focus on making Black Rock City a sustainable city improve people's perception of Burning Man? Why, or why not?
10. Could sustainable practices developed at Burning Man be used to help make everyone's default world behaviors better? Why, or why not?
